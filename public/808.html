JS-808
The JS-808 is an emulation of the Roland TR-808, drum machine that was first introduced in 1980 and has become a staple in the music industry with fans ranging from Tame Impala's Kevin Parker to Kanye West.
To get started:
1. Select a sound
2. Toggle edit and activate steps
3. Adjust volume
4. Adjust speed
4. Click the Start/Stop button!
